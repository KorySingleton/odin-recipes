Eggs and Rice

Desczription

A nice dish that's easy to fix and enjoy while not being heavily time consuming. You can also add a protein of your choice to the dish is desired

Ingredients

  • 4 whole eggs
  • 2 cups of rice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Meat of choice

Steps

  1. Take out, defrost and salt the protein of your choice
  2. Once the meat has defrosted, rinse and clean the rice
  3. Place the rice into a pot and fill with water until the level rises slightly above the rice. Then cover and place onto high heat until steaming
  4. Once the water starts steaming, lower to low heat and allow it to keep going until the water is completely gone
  5. While that is still on, pour some grease of your choice onto a pan over medium height and break the egg yolks into it, scrambling them. Simeoutaneously put on the protein choice.
  6. As the eggs finish, add them to the rice pot and cover it again, on low simmer until the protein finishes.
